ABOUT NEMESIS CYBER FORCE

Founded by Volkan Sah, Nemesis Cyber Force is a collective of security professionals dedicated to making the digital world safer through knowledge and action.

We believe security should be a fundamental concern for everyone, not just tech enthusiasts. Our community learns, collaborates, and builds tools that protect and empower.

We envision a safer digital future where people are aware of risks and take proactive measures to secure their digital lives.
